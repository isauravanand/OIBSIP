Biography

Virat Kohli, born November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India, is a
prominent cricketer known for his aggressive batting
style.He has captained the Indian cricket team and
holds numerous records in international cricket.Kohli

leadership on the field, earning him widespread
acclaim and adoration.
